## Edinburgh Medical School, CMVM

### Module
Research and Evidence Based Medicine (Year 1 and Year 2, MBChB)

### Academic year
2025-2026

### Notes
Teaching across both MBChB years.

### DRPS entries (2025-26)
REBM appears within the MBChB Foundations of Medicine courses rather than as a standalone DRPS course page.

- Year 1 context: HCP-Med: Foundations of Medicine 1 (MBCH08014)
- Year 2 context: HCP-Med: Foundations of Medicine 2 (MBCH09020)
- School/College: Edinburgh Medical School, College of Medicine and Veterinary Medicine
- Level/Credits: SCQF 8 (Year 1) and SCQF 9 (Year 2), each 90 credits (ECTS 45)
- Course start: Full Year
- DRPS URLs:
	- https://www.drps.ed.ac.uk/25-26/dpt/cxmbch08014.htm
	- https://www.drps.ed.ac.uk/25-26/dpt/cxmbch09020.htm
- Summary: REBM is described as part of the integrated teaching on evidence-based medicine, statistics/data handling, and assessed in-course work.

### DRPS synthesis
#### Learning objectives
- Build evidence-based medicine capability through critical appraisal and applied statistics/data handling.
- Integrate biomedical, socio-ethical, and evidence-informed reasoning in clinical contexts.
- Develop professional communication and reflective skills within longitudinal clinical placements.

#### Course content
- REBM content is embedded within the KCP/parallel strands of Foundations of Medicine 1 and 2.
- Teaching connects evidence-based medicine methods to biomedical blocks and real clinical scenarios.
- Statistics and data interpretation are included as part of the broader MBChB learning framework.

#### Assessment profile
- REBM is assessed through integrated in-course assignments within FoM1/FoM2 rather than a standalone exam paper.
- FoM1 overall assessment: 65% written exam, 35% coursework.
- FoM2 overall assessment: 50% written exam, 40% coursework, 10% practical.
