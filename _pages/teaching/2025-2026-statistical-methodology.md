## School of Mathematics, CSE

### Module
Statistical Methodology (Y3/4)

### Academic year
2025-2026

### Notes
Methods-focused teaching for senior undergraduate students.

### DRPS entry (2025-26)
- Course: Statistical Methodology (MATH10095)
- School/College: School of Mathematics, College of Science and Engineering
- Level/Credits: SCQF 10, 10 credits (ECTS 5)
- Course start: Semester 1
- DRPS URL: https://www.drps.ed.ac.uk/25-26/dpt/cxmath10095.htm
- Summary: Core likelihood-based statistical inference and linear modelling course used as preparation for advanced statistics options.

### DRPS synthesis
#### Learning objectives
- Apply likelihood-based inference for estimation, confidence intervals, and hypothesis testing.
- Fit and interpret normal linear models with attention to model assumptions.
- Conduct statistical analyses in R with appropriate workflow and reporting.

#### Course content
- Likelihood function, maximum likelihood estimation, and likelihood ratio testing.
- Bayesian foundations (Bayes theorem and posterior distributions).
- Iterative estimation methods (including Fisher scoring) and normal linear models.

#### Assessment profile
- 80% written exam.
- 20% coursework.
