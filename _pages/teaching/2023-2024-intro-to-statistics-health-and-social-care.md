## Usher Institute, CMVM

### Module
Introduction to Statistics for Health and Social Care

### Academic year
2023-2024

### Notes
Co-course organiser role in Semester 2.

### DRPS synthesis
#### Learning objectives
- Gain an introductory grasp of statistical ideas used in health and social care.
- Read and interpret basic numerical summaries and statistical outputs.

#### Course content
- Descriptive statistics, basic inference, and data interpretation.
- Health and social care examples that motivate statistical reasoning.

#### Assessment profile
- Coursework-led assessment built around applied statistical exercises.
