### DRPS synthesis
#### Learning objectives
- Understand machine learning applications and limitations in health and social care.
- Apply classification and regression techniques to complex, context-rich datasets.
- Distinguish supervised vs unsupervised methods and critically reflect on ethical implications.

#### Course content
- Introductory ML framing and differentiation from broader AI concepts.
- Supervised learning (classification and regression) and unsupervised learning (clustering/dimensionality).
- Python-based practical implementation with health and social care case-study datasets.

#### Assessment profile
- 100% coursework (final written assignment).
- No written exam.
