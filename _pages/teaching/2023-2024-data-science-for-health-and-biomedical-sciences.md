## Usher Institute, CMVM

### Module
Data Science for Health and Biomedical Sciences

### Academic year
2023-2024

### Notes
Undergraduate module support in R and data science.

### DRPS synthesis
#### Learning objectives
- Develop practical data science skills for biomedical contexts.
- Use R to manage, analyse, and present health-related data.

#### Course content
- Programming and data handling for health and biomedical sciences.
- Applied workflows for analysis, visualisation, and interpretation.

#### Assessment profile
- Assessed through coursework focused on practical data-science tasks.
