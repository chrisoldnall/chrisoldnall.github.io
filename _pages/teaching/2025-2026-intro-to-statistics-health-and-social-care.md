### DRPS entry (2025-26)
- Course: Introduction to statistics in health and social care (HEIN11039)
- School/College: Edinburgh Medical School, College of Medicine and Veterinary Medicine
- Level/Credits: SCQF 11, 10 credits (ECTS 5)
- Course type/start: Online distance learning, flexible start (from 05/01/2026)

- Summary: Introductory statistics for health and social care data science, combining theory and practical R-based analysis.

### DRPS synthesis
#### Learning objectives
- Develop critical understanding of core statistical concepts for health and social care.
- Apply basic statistical techniques in R to practical sector-relevant problems.
- Analyse, interpret, and communicate statistical findings to diverse audiences.

#### Course content
- Foundations: populations, samples, data measurement, and descriptive statistics.
- Probability distributions and statistical inference fundamentals.
- Applied methods including ANOVA, correlation, and regression using real health/social care datasets.

#### Assessment profile
- 100% coursework (final written assignment).
- No written exam.
