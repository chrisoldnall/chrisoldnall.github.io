## Edinburgh Medical School, CMVM

### Module
Research and Evidence Based Medicine (Year 1 and Year 2, MBChB)

### Academic year
2024-2025

### Notes
Teaching across both MBChB years.

### DRPS entries (2024-25)
REBM appears within the MBChB Foundations of Medicine courses rather than as a standalone DRPS course page.

- Year 1 context: HCP-Med: Foundations of Medicine 1 (MBCH08014)
- Year 2 context: HCP-Med: Foundations of Medicine 2 (MBCH09020)
- School/College: Edinburgh Medical School, College of Medicine and Veterinary Medicine
- Level/Credits: SCQF 8 (Year 1) and SCQF 9 (Year 2), each 90 credits (ECTS 45)
- Course start: Full Year
- DRPS URLs:
	- https://www.drps.ed.ac.uk/24-25/dpt/cxmbch08014.htm
	- https://www.drps.ed.ac.uk/24-25/dpt/cxmbch09020.htm
- Summary: REBM is included within the integrated MBChB curriculum and referenced in teaching and feedback components rather than listed as a separate DRPS course.

### DRPS synthesis
#### Learning objectives
- Build evidence-based medicine capability through critical appraisal and applied statistics/data handling.
- Integrate biomedical, socio-ethical, and evidence-informed reasoning in clinical contexts.
- Develop professional communication and reflective skills alongside longitudinal GP placement.

#### Course content
- REBM content is embedded in the Foundations of Medicine structure across Year 1 and Year 2.
- Teaching links evidence-based medicine methods to biomedical modules and clinical application.
- Data interpretation and statistics are taught within wider MBChB integration.

#### Assessment profile
- REBM is assessed through integrated in-course components rather than a standalone REBM exam.
- FoM1 overall assessment: 65% written exam, 35% coursework.
- FoM2 overall assessment: 50% written exam, 40% coursework, 10% practical.
