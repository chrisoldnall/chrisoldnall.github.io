## School of Mathematics, CSE

### Module
Facets of Mathematics (Y2)

### Academic year
2025-2026

### Notes
Year 2 support and delivery.

### DRPS entry (2025-26)
- Course: Facets of Mathematics (MATH08068)
- School/College: School of Mathematics, College of Science and Engineering
- Level/Credits: SCQF 8, 10 credits (ECTS 5)
- Course start: Semester 1
- DRPS URL: https://www.drps.ed.ac.uk/25-26/dpt/cxmath08068.htm
- Summary: Year 2 course covering three mathematical themes, with skills development in Python, mathematical writing, and LaTeX.

### DRPS synthesis
#### Learning objectives
- Demonstrate understanding across three distinct mathematical topic areas.
- Communicate mathematical ideas effectively using written and presentation formats.
- Use LaTeX for mathematical document preparation and Python for simple computational tasks.

#### Course content
- Three rotating mathematical themes drawn from areas such as pure, applied, and operational research mathematics.
- Application-focused exploration within each theme.
- Embedded skills strand in Python, LaTeX, and group project work.

#### Assessment profile
- 100% coursework.
- No written exam.
