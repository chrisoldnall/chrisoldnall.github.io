## PhD Industry Project

### Project title
Understanding the public health waiting times landscape in Scotland: finding key drivers and forecasting demand.

### Collaborators
With M. Brunet-Gusasch and S. Verhees.

### Summary
This project analysed Scottish cancer waiting times data to examine pandemic effects and identify key drivers of variation in referral and diagnosis patterns, using generalised linear models.
