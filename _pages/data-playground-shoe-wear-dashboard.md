## Why I built it

This project started as a practical question: how many miles do I get from each pair of shoes before support and comfort noticeably drop?

## What it tracks

- Pair identifier and purchase date
- Usage by activity type
- Estimated mileage and wear stage
- Notes on comfort and replacement timing

## Current status

I am iterating on the visual design and adding better trend summaries across shoe types and weekly activity.
